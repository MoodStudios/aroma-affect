# Aroma Affect Guide

**Scent-Powered Exploration for Minecraft**

## 1. Getting Started

### Welcome to Aroma Affect!

Aroma Affect brings the sense of smell into Minecraft. Your journey starts with the Aroma Guide — a compass that points you toward the nearest village where a Nose Smith awaits.

#### The Aroma Guide

Given to you on first join. Points to the nearest village.

Lost your guide? Craft a new one:

| | | |
|---|---|---|
| | | |
| Compass | Paper | |
| | | |

**Result:** Aroma Guide

#### The Nose Smith

Find the Nose Smith inside a village — a villager with an oversized nose. Talk to them, bring the flower they request, and they'll give you their nose as a reward: the Forager's Nose.

#### Noses & Scent Detection

Equip a Nose in your helmet slot to unlock scent abilities. Progression starts with villages and basic blocks, then flowers, and then full category tracking (including biomes) on higher tiers. Press R to open the Scent Menu.

#### OVR Hardware

Connect OVR scent hardware to experience real fragrances as you play. The mod works without hardware too — scents are always indicated visually.

To connect your OVR device, download the OVR Bridge — a companion app that links Minecraft with your scent hardware:

[Download OVR Bridge](https://www.ovrtechnology.com/)

> **Tip:** Once the Bridge is installed, check the connection status in Settings → Bridge within the mod's configuration screen.

## 3. Sniffer

### Overview

The Sniffer is the key to the Aroma Affect endgame. Once tamed, it becomes your loyal companion — ride it across dimensions, equip it with a powerful nose, and let it sniff out the rarest materials in the world.

> **Tip:** Finding a Sniffer Egg is easier with the Ancient Nose equipped. Its Precise Sniffer ability gives you a 40% chance to find a Sniffer Egg when sniffing Suspicious Sand.

#### What Can the Sniffer Do?

- Tame it with Torch Flowers and ride it with a Saddle
- Equip it with the Enhanced Sniffer Nose
- Take it on a Dimensional Tour to collect rare scents
- Once fully equipped, it produces Scent Base — the endgame crafting material

**Scent Base** — the endgame material produced by an equipped Sniffer

---

### Taming & Riding

#### How to Tame

Feed a Sniffer 4 Torch Flowers to tame it. Each flower advances the taming progress — you'll see heart particles when successful.

**Torch Flower** — feed 4 to tame a Sniffer

#### Riding

Once tamed, equip a Saddle on the Sniffer to ride it. Open the Sniffer's inventory with Shift + Right Click and place the Saddle in the first slot. Then right-click with an empty hand to mount.

**Saddle** — required in slot 1 for riding

#### Sniffer Inventory

The Sniffer has 2 equipment slots accessible via Shift + Right Click:

- **Slot 1:** Saddle — enables riding
- **Slot 2:** Sniffer Nose — enhances abilities

> **Tip:** Riding controls: standard movement keys. The Sniffer moves at 80% of its normal speed, with reduced strafe and reverse.

---

### Dimensional Tour

The Dimensional Tour is the quest to collect all three dimension scents. Take your tamed Sniffer to each dimension — it will sniff the air and produce a unique scent item the first time it visits each realm.

#### How It Works

When your Sniffer digs in a new dimension, it has an 85% chance to drop that dimension's scent instead of its usual seeds. Each scent can only be obtained once per Sniffer.

#### Dimension Scents

- **Overworld Scent** — obtained when the Sniffer digs in the Overworld
- **Nether Scent** — obtained when the Sniffer digs in the Nether
- **End Scent** — obtained when the Sniffer digs in the End

> **Tip:** Collecting all three scents grants the "Sniffer Journey" advancement and triggers a celebration with Totem of Undying particles!

Once you have all three scents, you can craft the Enhanced Sniffer Nose — the key to the endgame.

---

### Iron Nose

A rare collectible nose made of iron.

The Iron Nose is a unique item obtained from Iron Golems. It serves as the key crafting ingredient for the Enhanced Sniffer Nose.

#### How to Obtain

Drop a Special Rose near an Iron Golem. The golem will pick it up, give you its nose as an Iron Nose item, and permanently lose its nose.

**Special Rose** — purchase from the Nose Smith's shop

> **Tip:** Each Iron Golem can only give its nose once. Once taken, the golem's nose is gone forever!

---

### Enhanced Sniffer Nose

**100 Durability · Repair: Diamond**

The culmination of the Dimensional Tour. Crafted from all three dimension scents and an Iron Nose, this powerful nose transforms your Sniffer into an endgame resource machine.

#### Abilities

- **Scent Base Production** — the Sniffer produces Scent Base, the endgame crafting material
- **Enhanced Sniffing** — the Sniffer sniffs more frequently (0.5% chance per tick when idle)
- **Rare Drops** — finds diamonds, emeralds, ores, and other exclusive materials at random

**Scent Base** — produced by the Sniffer with this nose equipped

#### How to Equip

Open the Sniffer's inventory (Shift + Right Click) and place the Enhanced Sniffer Nose in the second slot (decoration slot). The Sniffer will immediately start using its enhanced abilities.

#### Recipe

| | | |
|---|---|---|
| | Overworld Scent | |
| Nether Scent | **Iron Nose** | End Scent |
| | Diamond | |

**Result:** Enhanced Sniffer Nose

> **Tip:** The Iron Nose is the center piece of this recipe. You'll need to befriend an Iron Golem first!
